IPv4-Only APN Profile Generator

Generate iOS profiles to disable IPv6 on cellular connections

What is this tool?

This tool creates a custom APN profile for iOS devices that forces your cellular connection to use IPv4 only, disabling IPv6 completely. Some users prefer this to avoid connectivity issues or improve performance on certain networks.

Note: This profile only affects cellular data connections and won't impact your Wi-Fi or other network interfaces.

How to Install

  1. Generate and download your profile
  2. Open the file on your iOS device (tap on it in Files, Mail, or Messages)
  3. When prompted, tap "Install" and enter your passcode
  4. The profile will be installed under Settings → General → VPN & Device Management
  5. Your device will now use IPv4 only for cellular data
